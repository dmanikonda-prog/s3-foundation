The Three Pillars of S3

✦

Every activity at S3 Foundation is rooted in these three timeless principles of Sanatana Dharma that guide our path toward individual and societal upliftment.

🙌

Seva

सेवा — Selfless Service

We believe that serving others is serving the Divine. Through meaningful volunteer work — from caring for Gir cows to community service — we transform individual hearts and uplift society at large.

🧘

Sadhana

साधना — Spiritual Practice

Disciplined spiritual practice is the core of transformation. Whether it is Agnihotra, Japa, Dhyana, or Yagna — we provide the sacred space and guidance for authentic Vedic sadhana.

🕉

Satsang

सत्संग — Spiritual Community

The company of truth-seekers elevates the soul. Our satsangs, discourses, and retreats create a sacred community where seekers of all backgrounds deepen their understanding of dharma.

A Vision Born from Ancient Dharma

"To uplift society through individual development rooted in Sanatana Dharma."

S3 Foundation (Shraddha Saburi Samidha) was founded in 2005 by students at the University of Memphis, inspired by Sri Purushottamji's vision to create an organization practicing ancient spiritual sadhana exactly as prescribed in our scriptures.

Over nearly two decades, the Foundation has grown into a flourishing spiritual campus in Arlington, Tennessee, hosting hundreds of sadhakas, performing grand Yagnas, and nurturing over 200 sacred Gir cows on 400 acres of blessed land.

S3 Gaushala — Sacred Cow Sanctuary

Our Gaushala in Arlington, Tennessee is home to 200+ pure-bred Gir cows — a breed revered in Vedic tradition for their spiritual and healing qualities. Spread across 400+ acres, it is one of the most authentic Vedic Gaushalas in the United States.

🐄

200+ Authentic Gir Cows

Pure-bred Gir cows from India, lovingly cared for with traditional Vedic practices on expansive natural pastures.

🌿

Cow-Based Organic Farming

Sustainable farming using Panchagavya (five cow products) — fulfilling the ancient vision of self-sufficient Vedic village life.

🔥

Daily Agnihotra

The sacred Vedic fire ritual performed every day at sunrise and sunset, purifying the land and the hearts of all who attend.
