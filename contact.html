How to Get Here

🚗 By Car (from downtown Memphis)

Take I-40 W toward Arlington. Exit at Canada Rd / TN-388. Head north, then turn right onto Deadfall Road. S3 Gaushala is approximately 30 minutes from downtown Memphis. Look for the entrance sign on the left side.

✈️ Nearest Airport

Memphis International Airport (MEM) is approximately 35 minutes away. Rental car service is recommended. Please coordinate with us for group shuttle arrangements for large events.

🏨 Nearby Accommodation

For multi-day events, we have a guesthouse on campus for registered sadhakas. Several hotels are also available in Arlington and Bartlett, TN — contact us for recommendations.
