Our Mission

"To uplift society through individual development rooted in Sanatana Dharma."

S3 Foundation exists to create a living bridge between ancient Vedic wisdom and the modern world — providing every sincere seeker, regardless of background, with access to authentic spiritual practices, Vedic knowledge, and a sacred community of dharmic companions.

The Name & Meaning

What Does S3 Stand For?

Three Sanskrit words that capture the entire spiritual journey.

The name S3 represents the foundational triad of spiritual life as described in Sanatana Dharma. Each 'S' is a pillar — and together, they form the complete path from aspiration to liberation.

🙌
Shraddha — श्रद्धा

Faith. The sincere, heartfelt belief in the Divine, in the Guru, and in the sacred texts. Shraddha is the very seed of the spiritual journey.

🧘
Saburi — सबुरी

Patience. The steadfast, patient perseverance on the spiritual path — trusting in divine timing and remaining unwavering through all of life's challenges.

🔥
Samidha — समिधा

Sacred Offering. The sacred wood offered into the Vedic fire — symbolizing the complete offering of the self to the Divine through seva and yajna.

Our Journey

20 Years of Dharmic Growth

✦

From a small student organization to a 400-acre Vedic campus — our timeline of faith and service.

2005
Foundation Established
S3 Foundation was founded by students at the University of Memphis, inspired by Sri Purushottamji's vision for authentic Vedic spiritual practice in the USA.
2008
First Grand Yagna
Successfully organized the first Gayatri Yagna and Maha Mrutyunjaya Homa, drawing hundreds of devotees from across the region.
2012
Land Acquisition
Acquired 400+ acres of pristine land in Arlington, Tennessee — the foundation of the future Vedic campus and Gaushala.
2015
Gaushala Inaugurated
S3 Gaushala was inaugurated with the arrival of the first Gir cows from India — a landmark moment for Gau Mata Seva in the USA.
2018
Yagna Shala Completed
The magnificent 30,000 sq.ft Yagna Shala was completed, enabling grand Vedic rituals with proper traditional infrastructure.
2020
Laksha Chandi Maha Yagnam
Organized the landmark Laksha (100,000) Chandi Maha Yagnam during Navaratri — an unprecedented Vedic event in the Americas.
2023
Shiva Shakti Maha Yagnam
Conducted the Shiva Shakti Maha Yagnam with thousands of devotees participating, further establishing S3 as a premier Vedic center in North America.
2025+
Continuing the Mission
200+ Gir cows, ongoing daily Agnihotra, residential sadhana programs, and expansion of the Vedic campus — the journey continues.

Our Core Values

✦
🕉

Dharma First

Every action is guided by the principles of Sanatana Dharma — eternal truths that transcend time, culture, and geography.

🌿

Ahimsa

Non-violence toward all beings — from our cherished Gir cows to every visitor. Our campus is a sanctuary of compassion.

🤝

Inclusivity

All programs are free and open to every sincere seeker regardless of caste, creed, nationality, or background.

📖

Authentic Tradition

We follow Vedic rituals and practices precisely as prescribed in ancient scriptures — preserving the original living tradition.

🌱

Sustainability

Cow-based organic farming, zero-waste practices, and deep respect for Bhumi Mata (Mother Earth) in all our activities.

🎓

Knowledge

Sharing Vedic wisdom through satsangs, discourses, workshops, and direct transmission from learned Gurus and Acharyas.

💚

Community

Building a thriving dharmic community where families, students, and seekers support each other on the spiritual path.

🙏

Humility

Recognizing that we are instruments of the Divine. All credit belongs to the blessings of Gurus and the grace of the Divine.

Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Have more questions? We're happy to help — reach out to us at any time.

Contact Us
Is S3 Foundation open to everyone? +
Yes! S3 Foundation is open to all sincere seekers of truth regardless of religion, caste, nationality, or background. All our programs are free of charge to sadhakas.
How can I visit the Gaushala? +
Gaushala visits are available through scheduled tours. Please contact us in advance to arrange your visit. The Gaushala is located at 7945 Deadfall Road, Arlington, Tennessee.
Can I stay at the Gaushala for sadhana? +
Yes! We have a guesthouse available for sadhakas who wish to stay and do intensive sadhana. Accommodation is modest and spiritually focused. Please contact us to check availability.
How can I volunteer? +
We welcome volunteers for cow care, kitchen seva, event organization, and administrative support. Fill out our volunteer form on the Contact page or email us directly.
Is S3 Foundation a registered nonprofit? +
Yes, S3 Foundation is a registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization. All donations are tax-deductible to the extent permitted by law.
