Our Sacred Sanctuary

A Home for Gau Mata in America

"Gavo vishvasya mataraha — The cow is the mother of the universe."

In Vedic tradition, the cow is considered sacred — a symbol of abundance, dharma, and compassion. At S3 Gaushala in Arlington, Tennessee, we honor this tradition by providing a loving, authentic Vedic home for over 200 pure-bred Gir cows from India.

Our Gaushala is not merely a farm — it is a sacred space where ancient knowledge meets modern land stewardship. Guided by Vedic principles, we practice cow-based organic farming using Panchagavya products, perform daily Agnihotra, and offer the healing presence of these gentle beings to all who visit.

About Our Cows

The Sacred Gir Cow

✦

We house authentic Gir cows — one of India's oldest and most revered Desi (indigenous) breeds, originating from the Gir forests of Gujarat.

🐄

Authentic Desi Breed

Gir cows (Bos Indicus) are an indigenous Indian breed prized for millennia for their gentle nature, high-quality A2 milk, and spiritual significance in Hindu tradition.

🥛

A2 Milk — Nature's Gift

Our Gir cows produce pure A2 milk containing A2 beta-casein protein — the original form of milk that is gentler on human digestion and considered superior in Ayurvedic science.

🌿

Free-Range Natural Life

Our cows graze freely on 400+ acres of pesticide-free natural pasture, fed with organic fodder and lovingly cared for by dedicated Gaushala sevaks year-round.

📿

Daily Gau Puja

Every morning, our Gir cows receive Vedic worship — anointed with sacred substances, adorned with garlands, and offered prayers as Gau Mata, the mother of the universe.

Gau Products

Panchagavya — The Five Sacred Products

✦

The five products of the sacred cow — together forming Panchagavya — have been used for thousands of years in Ayurveda, agriculture, and Vedic rituals.

🥛

Milk (Dugdha)

Pure A2 milk from our Gir cows — sattvic, nutritious, and the foundation of all dairy products on our campus.

🧈

Ghee (Ghrita)

Clarified butter made from our Gir cow milk — used in Agnihotra, Ayurvedic medicine, and as a sacred food offering.

🍶

Curd (Dadhi)

Naturally fermented curd rich in probiotics — used in Panchagavya preparations and as an offering in puja ceremonies.

💧

Cow Urine (Gomutra)

Considered medicinal in Ayurveda — used in traditional formulations, organic pesticides, and ritual purification.

🌱

Cow Dung (Gobar)

Rich organic fertilizer and fuel — used in Agnihotra, as building material, and to create superb compost for our organic farm.

🌿

Panchagavya Mix

The combined preparation of all five products — a powerful natural growth promoter for plants and soil-health booster used throughout our farm.
